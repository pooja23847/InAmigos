Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), InAmigos Foundation began with one belief: collective action resheps society.

Headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, our work now spans the entire nation.

Section 8 80G & 12A CSR-1 NITI Aayog ISO 9001:2015
Who We Are

A trusted foundation,

Our Work in Pictures
Our Initiatives

Six projects. One mission.

Each project targets a specific gap in society — together they form a complete ecosystem of care.

🍱
Food & Clothing

Project SEVA

Providing nutritious meals and clothing to the underprivileged across India. No one should go hungry or cold — SEVA makes that a daily reality.

🎯 50,000+ meals & clothing items distributed
📚
Education

Project BACHPANSHALA

Bridging educational gaps by equipping underprivileged children with digital literacy, life skills, and academic support to shape their future.

🎯 Children across India gaining quality education
🐾
Animal Welfare

Project JEEV

Dedicated to the welfare of stray animals — our volunteers feed, rescue, and protect animals who cannot speak for themselves.

🎯 50+ stray animals fed daily by volunteers
👩
Women Empowerment

Project UDAAN

Collaborating with rural self-help groups to promote financial independence, skill development, and menstrual hygiene awareness for women.

🎯 Rural women achieving financial independence
🌱
Environment

Project PRAKRITI

Advocating for sustainability through large-scale plantation drives and supporting eco-friendly farming in rural communities.

🎯 20,000+ saplings planted across India
💼
Skill Development

Project VIKAS

Facilitating employment through internships in data operations, finance, content writing, digital marketing, social work, and more.

🎯 30,000+ interns trained in the last 4 years
